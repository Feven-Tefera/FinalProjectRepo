    Welcome to LONDON Cafe Satellite,


    A premier dining destination bringing the flavors of modern European cuisine to the heart of Ethiopia. Established in 2020, our restaurant is committed to delivering an exceptional culinary experience that celebrates the art of fine dining and the vibrant culture of our local community. Nestled in the bustling city of Addis Ababa, LONDON Cafe Satellite offers a sophisticated ambiance where guests can indulge in a curated selection of contemporary European dishes, expertly crafted by our team of talented chefs. Drawing inspiration from the gastronomic traditions of the United Kingdom, our menu features a harmonious blend of classic and innovative flavors, showcasing the finest locally sourced ingredients and the culinary creativity of our kitchen. At the core of our establishment is a deep-rooted passion for hospitality. Our attentive and knowledgeable staff strive to provide personalized service, ensuring that every visit to LONDON Cafe Satellite is a memorable one. Whether you join us for an intimate dinner, a business luncheon, or a celebratory gathering, we are committed to exceeding your expectations and delivering a dining experience that leaves a lasting impression.




    In addition to our renowned restaurant, LONDON Cafe Satellite also offers a comprehensive catering service, tailoring our culinary expertise to cater for a wide range of events, from elegant private parties to corporate functions. Our dedicated catering team works closely with clients to design customized menus that cater to diverse palates and dietary requirements, ensuring that every event is a resounding success. At LONDON Cafe Satellite, we take pride in our commitment to sustainability and supporting the local community. We source our ingredients from trusted Ethiopian suppliers, fostering partnerships that contribute to the growth and development of the regional economy. Furthermore, we strive to minimize our environmental impact through eco-friendly practices and sustainable initiatives. We invite you to embark on a culinary journey with us at LONDON Cafe Satellite, where the timeless elegance of European cuisine meets the vibrant flavors of Ethiopia. Let us be your gateway to an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the art of gastronomy and the warmth of genuine hospitality.
